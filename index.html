Open Your Heart, Change a Life: Foster a Rescue Pet Today

Every year, thousands of loving animals enter shelters waiting for a second chance. By opening your home to a foster pet, you provide a safe, nurturing environment that prepares them for their forever family while freeing up critical space to save another life.

Start Your Fostering Journey

Why Fostering Matters

Fostering is a vital bridge between a rescue animal's past life and their bright future. Many pets struggle in a noisy shelter environment; by welcoming them into a quiet home, you help them decompress, reveal their true personalities, and learn essential socialization skills.

Whether you can host an animal for a weekend or a few months, your support gives rescue organizations the valuable time and space needed to rescue more animals in danger.

Fostering FAQs

Who pays for medical care and food?

PetQuest covers 100% of all veterinary approved medical costs, medication, and core supplies including crates and food sheets.

Can I foster if I already have personal pets?

Yes! We match foster animals based on your current household dynamic. We will ensure the rescue animal is compatible with your resident pets prior to placement.
